Project Overview

Overview

Application Purpose: Website to promote advertisement for a drone videography business.

Intended Users: People who want to hire an aerial drone videographer for events.

Content Overview

Home Page

  • Introduce the site and catch the user’s attention.
  • Highlight a featured video (usually recent).
  • Small bio.

About Page

  • Bio of the videographer, drone pilot, and editor.

Portfolio Page

  • Showcase the videographer’s work.
  • Gallery of videos and edits.
  • Gallery with embedded YouTube videos.

Services Page

  • Information about the videographer’s services.
  • List of services and pricing details.

Contact Page

  • Communication for potential clients to get in touch.
  • Name, email, and phone number fields.

Page Design

Home

  • Purpose: Introduce the site and encourage visitors to explore or contact them.
  • Audience/Users: Potential clients; people interested in hiring a drone videographer.
  • Content: Small introduction, welcome statement.
  • Will users be asked to enter data? No.
  • Contains buttons or hyperlinks? Possibly.

About

  • Purpose: Provide background information about the videographer.
  • Audience/Users: People interested in hiring a videographer.
  • Content: Professional bio.
  • Will users be asked to enter data? No.
  • Contains buttons or hyperlinks? A button linking to the Contact page.

Portfolio

  • Purpose: Showcase their work.
  • Audience/Users: Potential clients.
  • Content: Gallery of videos and edits with captions/descriptions.
  • Will users be asked to enter data? No.
  • Contains buttons or hyperlinks? Yes, links to YouTube videos.

Services

  • Purpose: Inform visitors about event coverage and pricing.
  • Audience/Users: Potential clients wanting service details.
  • Content: List of services and prices.
  • Will users be asked to enter data? No.
  • Contains buttons or hyperlinks? Optional button linking to Contact page.

Contact

  • Purpose: Provide a way for clients to contact them.
  • Audience/Users: Potential clients wanting to book or ask questions.
  • Content: Phone number and email form fields.
  • Will users be asked to enter data? Yes, via a form.
  • Contains buttons or hyperlinks? No.

Dynamic Functionality

Embedded Video Links on Portfolio Page

When users click a video, it will play within the site, allowing access to content without leaving the page.

Why: Viewers can see high-quality videos without switching to YouTube.

Example: https://avada.com/element/youtube/

Sticky Navigation Bar

A navigation bar remains at the top of the site regardless of the page.

Why: Users can quickly access navigation options at all times.
