Website Evaluations

⟢ Airbnb

Airbnb’s homepage is known for its clean design, intuitive search bar, and strong use of visual hierarchy. The site emphasizes usability with clear calls-to-action and a cohesive aesthetic that aligns well with modern UI principles.

  • Contrast: The main search bar stands out clearly against the white background with bold text and icons.
  • Repetition: Fonts, button styles, and card layouts are consistent throughout the site, creating a uniform look.
  • Alignment: Content is arranged in a clean grid, with clear alignment of images, text, and buttons.
  • Proximity: Related information (like property photo, price, and rating) is grouped together, making browsing intuitive.

⟢ LingsCars

LingsCars is infamous for its chaotic, over-the-top design. While it may attract attention, the site often overwhelms users with flashing graphics, clashing colors, and scattered elements that violate CRAP principles.

  • Contrast: The color palette is excessive and chaotic, reducing readability instead of highlighting important features.
  • Repetition: Fonts, styles, and visual elements change constantly, creating a disjointed experience.
  • Alignment: Elements appear misaligned or randomly placed, making the layout feel unstructured.
  • Proximity: Content is cluttered together with little logical grouping, forcing users to scan chaotically to find what they need.
